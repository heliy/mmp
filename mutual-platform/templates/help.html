ABOUT

This is a mutual platform for students and staffs in school.
For users in this platform, you can post your tasks and others will help you.
And, if you is a staff, you can post a notice to all students in this platform.

USEAGE

Register

There are there steps for you to register a account.
1. Input your phone number
2. Valid your phone number
3. Complete your account information

You have to enter unique username, your student number and your address.
Least but not last, Your password.

You MUST remember your phone number and password to sign in.

Task & Tag

Tasks in this platform is arranged by Tags.
So, if you want to post your task in some topic, please make sure you choose the related tags.
Of course, you can create a new tag by yourself.

For post a task, you have to enter the title and describtions about it.
Besides Tags, you also need enter the deadline of your task.
If the user who help you are not complete task before deadline, this task would FAILED.
Moreover, if it have not received by anyone, this task will be CLOSED.

If you want to receive the task posted by others,
you can hit the 'Receive' submit under the task before another person get that task.
After receive the task, and complete that task, you should hit the 'Complete' submit.
Or, if you want to abort the task, you also should hit the 'Abort' submit.

If the task is complete, or aborted by helper, or just pass the deadline,
the task will not order other actions except commit the users each other.
You need mark a score between 0 and 5 to your partner.
And average of those scores will be the score you get, and be showed in your homepage.

If you is a staff and need post a notice, you need click the 'Post Notice' in index page.

Show

Under different tags, the tasks will be listed order by create date.
Also, there will be other specifiec show page.
  • Urgent Tasks show the tasks that have not received by anyone and have less 6 hours before deadline.
  • Recent Tasks show the tasks that active in one day.
  • Popular Tasks show the tasks that hit most by users.
